Manu & Koushu Arts is a creative YouTube channel dedicated to the beautiful world of paper crafts, origami, and DIY projects. We believe everyone can be an artist — all you need is paper, patience, and a little imagination.

From beginner-friendly tutorials to stunning origami masterpieces, our videos make crafting accessible, fun and rewarding for people of all ages.
